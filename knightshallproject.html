Knights Hall




Østfyns Museer asked us to create a powerful and learningful Virtual Reality experience for Nyborg Castle.

Inside you find yourself standing within the Knights Hall of Nyborg Castle. Here you are able to interact with a miniature model of the castle, complete with points of interest of which you can read and learn about

You are also able to switch between the castle as it looked in the year 1200 AD and 1500 AD.
