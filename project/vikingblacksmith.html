The viking blacksmith

We created this Viking blacksmith VR experience for a Danish vikingmuseum, in collaboration with "Den fynske spilfabrik."

The experience brings you back to the viking age, where you are able to forge your very own blade using a hammer and anvil.

Heating up the metal and operating the bellow are part of what makes this experience realistic and informative.

The Viking Blacksmith has been a provisional attraction in two different vikingmuseums in Denmark.

The game was originally developed for HTC Vive, but has been ported to Oculus Rift and further improved
