City Of Thieves

City of Thieves is our first original IP

It is a board game for four players set in a medieval city. The city is controlled by four gangs of thieves, that robs the city's shops every night.

It is a semi-casual, yet fun, strategic and family friendly game. The appealing and fun art- and playstyle is developed entirely by our team.

The game is set for release in spring 2018, and will be published by "Game Inventors"
