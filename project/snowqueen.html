The Snow Queen

In collaboration with the acclaimed film company Zentropa, we created a virtual reality experience to promote their upcoming movie "The snow queen".

Inside the VR experience you find yourself in the icey cave of the Snow Queen. Trapped in a cage of icicles, with no way out, you are forced to listen to the Snow Queen taunt you as she slowly makes her way towards you.

If you try to escape by breaking the icicles, they will grow back instantly, you are truly forsaken

In the end, she drains the lifeforce out of you and you will drop dead on the spot.

The experience was launched with a grand opening covered by the media. Peter Ålbæk attended the event and praised the products and it's capabilities in a interview with the Danish TV2.

VR makes a great marketing and promotion tool
