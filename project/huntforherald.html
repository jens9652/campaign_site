The hunt for harald

"VIFIN is the organization behind "The hunt for Harald" - an immersive language and culture simulation that teaches the user through speech and interactions.

Artifact Game Solutions were tasked to restore and update the entire game in Unity and port to WebGL and Android. The game also received minor visual improvements along with quality of life gameplay updates and technical upgrades.

VIFIN also offers language and culture training simulations for au pair and social/health workers.

We implemented the gameplay and experience in these two games, as well as optimization, sound design and minor asset creation.

The games help you understand and learn through spoken instructions and small tasks.
