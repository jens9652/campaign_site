kidnapning

"Kidnapning" is a Danish family movie, from the production company Regner Grasten.

The movie premiered in July 2017 in cinemas all over Denmark and Artifact Game Solutions helped promote the movie before and during launch.

7 minigames based on the movie and its characters where created to be played on the website and by the actors in promotional videos across all social media.
