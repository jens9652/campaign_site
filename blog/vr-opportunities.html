Virtual Reality and it's opportunities

by Patrick Johnson the 19-02-2018

How can Virtual Reality alter the way we look at museum experiences? How does it affect the audience? That's some of the question we will be answering in this blog post. We have created a promotion video featuring some of the possibilities with Virtual Reality.

Virtual Reality is really getting into the world of museums, but the experince is not optimal for the audience. Most exhibitions today that incorprates Virtual Reality has no or very little incorporation of gamification. We think this is sad, as it is where Virtual Reality really can shine through. Cultural Museums and Science Centers are some of the targets for a really good experience in Virtual Reality and is where some of our largest and best projects comes from.

We have created this video to show the possibilites of Virtual Reality and how it can enrich the experience of visiting a museum. We want to inspire our clients into choosing VR as a platform for learning.

Games and art has won our hearts long ago - are you ready to join a Virtual Reality revolution?
